Tara Templin

I am currently a researcher at the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, working on the Financial Resources for Health Team. My interests include the allocation and effectiveness of government expenditure and development assistance, from both theoretical and empirical perspectives.

My current projects involve forecasting health expenditure for each country by payer to 2040 and estimating the number of people living below the international poverty threshold.
